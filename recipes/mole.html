Mole

Ingredients

  • To Cook the Chicken
  • 1 whole chicken (5 lb.), cut into 8 pieces
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ¼ white onion
  • 1 celery stick
  • 2 parsley or cilantro sprigs
  • 8 cups of water
  • Salt to taste
  • For the Mole Sauce
  • 5 oz. tomatoes
  • 1 white onion, cut into thick slices
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • 4–5 cups chicken broth
  • 1 toasted corn tortilla*
  • 1 jar (8.25 oz.) DOÑA MARÍA® Mole
  • ½ tablet Mexican chocolate**
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon ground anise seed
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds (to garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Chicken:
    1. Place the chicken pieces in a stockpot with the garlic, onion, celery stick (cut into 2–3 pieces), and parsley (or cilantro). Cover with water.
    2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce heat and simmer 35–45 minutes until tender.
    3. Season with salt and set aside. Reserve the broth to dilute the mole paste.
  2. Prepare the Mole Sauce:
    1. While the chicken cooks, place the tomato, onion, and garlic in a saucepan. Cover with water and bring to a rolling boil.
    2. Reduce heat and cook about 8 minutes until tomatoes are soft.
    3. Drain and blend the tomatoes, onion, garlic, and toasted tortilla until smooth.
    4. In a large frying pan over low heat, add the DOÑA MARÍA® Mole and 1 cup chicken broth to begin diluting.
    5. Add another cup of broth along with the blended sauce, chocolate, cinnamon, and anise. Stir until fully dissolved.
    6. Add more broth as needed to reach a thick gravy consistency.
    7. Add the chicken pieces, season with salt, and simmer gently for about 5 minutes.
